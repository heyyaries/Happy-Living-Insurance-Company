What is Health Insurance?

Health insurance is a type of insurance coverage that pays for medical and surgical expenses incurred by the insured.

Types of Health Insurance

  • Individual Health Insurance
  • Family Floater Health Insurance
  • Critical Illness Insurance
  • Top-Up Plans
  • Group Health Insurance

How to Choose Health Insurance

When choosing health insurance, consider the following factors:

  1. Coverage Options
  2. Premium Costs
  3. Network Hospitals
  4. Claim Settlement Ratio
  5. Customer Reviews

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is a deductible?

A: A deductible is the amount you pay for covered health care services before your insurance plan starts to pay.

Q: What is a copayment?

A: A copayment is a fixed amount you pay for a covered health care service, usually when you receive the service.
