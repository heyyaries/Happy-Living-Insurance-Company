Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I file a claim?

A: Log into your Happy Living account and navigate to the claims section. Our customer service team is also available to assist you.

Q: Can I change my plan later?

A: Yes! You can review and change your plan during the open enrollment period or if you experience a qualifying life event.
